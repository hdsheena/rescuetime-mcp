## Data Conversion Scope

### Phase 1: Core Records (Completed)
- **Client/Contact Information** - Full history, addresses, phone, email
- **Animal/Pet Records** - Species, breed, age, weight tracking
- **Appointments** - Schedule, duration, provider assignment

### Phase 2: Clinical Data (In Progress / Post-Launch)
- **Visit History** - Complete encounter records with timestamps
- **Vitals** - Temperature, heart rate, respiratory data from all visits
- **Vaccines** - Immunization records with dates, types, lot numbers
- **Rabies Tags** - Tag number, issue date, expiration tracking
- **Reminders/SOC** - Standing orders, care reminders, follow-up flags
- **Products Administered** - Medications, dosages, dates, provider records
- **Charges & Invoices** - Transaction history, item-level billing, payment status
- **Attachments & Documents** - Medical files, lab results, notes

**Total Data Entities:** 8 major record types with cross-references
**Complexity:** Multi-system field mapping, date/time standardization, relational data integrity
